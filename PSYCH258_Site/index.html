Textbook Erratas

  • Note: Depending on which printing you have; some errata may have been corrected in newer printings.
  • • p.21: “12. Event after the shift in psychology...” should be “12. Even after the shift in psychology...”
  • • p.39-40: the textbook describes one kind of prosopagnosia; however, there are at least two different types, as described in the lectures
  • • p.83: in Figure 3.31 “What/Pereption” should be “What/Perception”
  • • p.120:- “Thus, participants had to divide their a ttention across two tasks...” should be “Thus, participants had to divide their attention across two tasks...”
  • • p.167: “As for the Fruits group,...” should be “As for the Professions group,...”
  • • p.182: “When you are painting with watercolor, how might you remove excess pain?” should be “...remove excess paint?”
  • • p.184: “(2) a conditioning stimulus” is more commonly called an “unconditioned stimulus”
  • • p.221: “Systems consolidation involves the gradual recognition of neural circuits.” should be “Systems consolidation involves the gradual reorganization of neural circuits.”
  • • p.225:- “...the Department of justice now requires that interrogations by recorded.” should be “...the Department of Justice now requires that interrogations be recorded.”
  • • p.231: “...amygdala activity was higher for the emotional words...” should be “...emotional pictures...”
  • • p.288: “Figure 9.24a shows a map that extends over a large area of the cortex, which indicates were specific words activate the cortex.” should be “Figure 9.24a shows a map that extends over a large area of the cortex, which indicates where specific words activate the cortex.”
  • • p.340 “However, participants responded ‘yes’ more rapidly when the picture’s orientation matched the situation described in the picture” should read “...matched the situation described in the sentence”
  • • p.388 (twice): “mediation” should be “meditation”
  • • p.431: “Cryptoamnesia” should be “Cryptomnesia”
  • • p.462: “McNeil, J. E., & Warrington, E. K. (1993). Prospoganosia: A face-specific disorder.” should be “McNeil, J. E., & Warrington, E. K. (1993). Prosopagnosia: A face-specific disorder.”

  • • In chapter 2, the caption for Figure 2.5 “...the resting potential, of 270 millivolts (mv)...” should be “-70 millivolts”
